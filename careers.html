Featured Position

Junior Supervisor
of Worldly Affairs

Mammon LLC, a leading player in the infernal business world, is seeking a Junior Supervisor of Worldly Affairs to join our team. As a Junior Supervisor of Worldly Affairs, you will be responsible for overseeing the operations of our worldly ventures — from manipulating stock prices to causing natural disasters.

Responsibilities

  • Planning and executing diabolical schemes to achieve our corporate objectives across all mortal territories and jurisdictions
  • Monitoring and manipulating global markets to increase our profits; experience with infernal arbitrage a distinct advantage
  • Manipulating natural events to our advantage — meteorological, geological, and other elemental disruptions as directed by the Patron
  • Managing the minions of the abyss in the mortal realm, including performance reviews, scheduling, and the annual Minion Excellence Awards
  • Compiling and submitting regular reports to your designated manager via occult ritual (see Appendix E: Reporting Obligations)

Qualifications

  • A deep understanding of the dark arts, including demonology, necromancy, and blood magic — intermediate proficiency minimum; mastery preferred
  • Strong leadership skills to manage the hordes of the abyss, including conflict resolution, delegation, and infernal motivational techniques
  • Ability to work well under pressure and to multitask in a fast-paced environment; the end of days is a demanding timeline
  • Willingness to forfeit one's soul to the Abyss for the sake of Mammon LLC's continued success — this is non-negotiable
  • Familiarity with the Laws of the Abyss and applicable mortal regulatory frameworks preferred
  • 10 Years of experience.
  • Proficiency in at least one eldritch invocation (see Appendix D for a full list of approved spells)
