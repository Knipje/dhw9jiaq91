About Mammon LLC

A diversified infernal holding company committed to excellence in chaos, greed, and soul management.

Mission & Values

Driven by Avarice,
Guided by the Patron

Mammon LLC was established under the eternal mandate of our Patron to exploit the full productive potential of mortal greed, despair, and ambition. We believe that every soul has value — and that it is our sacred corporate duty to acquire as many of them as possible, on the most advantageous terms achievable.

Our mission is simple: to advance the interests of the Abyss through disciplined, data-driven diabolism — while maintaining a positive, inclusive workplace culture for warlocks and demons of all backgrounds.

We operate across five core practice areas: financial disruption, elemental event management, warlock contract services, soul acquisition, and what we call Sustained Mortal Despair — a proprietary long-term investment strategy that consistently outperforms conventional benchmarks.

Our Patron's vision is our north star. Or south star, as the infernal navigation preferred by our board tends to orient toward the abyss rather than any fixed celestial body.

⛧   Uncompromising Greed

We pursue profit above all things, including ethics, mortality, and several laws of physics.

📋   Rigorous Process

Our pacts are ironclad. Our fine print is impenetrable. Our CR procedures are surprisingly thorough.

🌍   Global Impact

We operate across all mortal territories, infernal planes, and several jurisdictions that do not yet have names.

🦷   Employee Wellbeing

Competitive salary in Eldritch Blast, free dental, and access to arcane knowledge beyond mortal comprehension.

🤝   Inclusive Culture

We welcome beings of all races, genders, and planes of origin. Equal opportunity across all levels of evil.

🔒   Absolute Confidentiality

The Patron's true nature shall remain undisclosed. We do not elaborate on this further. Do not ask.
