Eldritch Pact
Mammon Incorporated LLC
This Eldritch Pact ("Contract") is entered into between the undersigned Warlock ("Warlock") and the elusive entity known as the Patron ("Patron"), hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Parties".
Consideration from the Patron

In consideration of the services provided by the Warlock, the Patron agrees to provide:

  1. Access to occult knowledge and arcane powers, including but not limited to the "Eldritch Blast", to be used at the Warlock's discretion.
  2. Assistance in achieving the Warlock's personal goals, subject to the discretion of the Patron.
  3. Provision of resources and protection from potential adversaries, as deemed necessary by the Patron.
  4. Reimbursement for reasonable and necessary dental expenses incurred by the Warlock, up to a pre-approved amount of gold pieces per year, upon submission of valid receipts.
Obligations of the Warlock

The Warlock agrees to perform the following services on behalf of the Patron:

  1. Utilise the supernatural powers granted by the Patron to cast spells, including but not limited to the "Eldritch Blast", for the Patron's benefit.
  2. Keep the existence of the Patron and the nature of their relationship strictly confidential, refraining from any disclosure to third parties.
  3. Fulfil any additional requests or tasks as directed by the Patron, without hesitation or question.
Governing Law & Termination

This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the Laws of the Abyss, without regard to its conflict of law principles. This Contract shall commence upon signing and remain in effect indefinitely, unless terminated by either Party with written notice. Termination shall not release the Warlock from obligations, including the obligation to maintain confidentiality.

In consideration of the powers and benefits bestowed upon the Warlock by the Patron, the Warlock agrees to transfer ownership and possession of their soul to the Patron in perpetuity, in accordance with the following key terms:

  • Soul transference is irrevocable and binding. There is no right to rescind or nullify once executed.
  • Upon completion, the Warlock's soul becomes the exclusive property of the Patron, to be used or disposed of at the Patron's sole discretion.
  • The Warlock represents and warrants that they have the legal capacity to enter into this agreement.
  • The soul transference is not contingent upon the Patron providing any specific services or benefits.

The Warlock acknowledges and understands the significance and consequences of the soul transference agreement, and voluntarily waives any and all claims or defences that may arise.

Additional Contract

Soul Renting Option

The Warlock may, solely at their discretion, choose to rent back their soul for a limited period of time — subject to the following conditions:

  1. Provide written notice to the Patron clearly indicating intention to rent.
  2. Agree upon a rental period in writing, not exceeding [insert duration] from the date of written notice.
  3. Relinquish and transfer to the Patron half of all mortal possessions — including wealth, property, and material assets — upon signing.
  4. Acknowledge that during the rental period, the soul remains the property of the Patron. The Warlock shall not engage in activities that may harm, damage, or alter the soul.
  5. Acknowledge that failure to transfer agreed-upon possessions results in immediate termination of the rental agreement.

Pact Appendices

The following appendices are incorporated by reference into all Eldritch Pacts. The Warlock is encouraged to seek clarification before signing. The Patron is not liable for failure to read the fine print.

Appendix A

Inclusive Language in the Eldritch Workspace

+

It is important for the Warlock to be mindful of using inclusive language in all interactions within the Eldritch workspace. The following guidelines apply:

  • Use gender-neutral terms — "all beings", "all creatures", "everyone" — unless otherwise directed by the Patron.
  • Respect cultural diversity across all planes of origin and avoid language offensive to other belief systems, however mortal.
  • Use preferred names and pronouns of all beings in the Eldritch workspace. Assumptions based on appearance or supernatural emanation are discouraged.
  • Avoid discriminatory language, slurs, or offensive terms regarding race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, plane of origin, or soul-possession status.
  • Be open to feedback. Ongoing awareness is required. The Eldritch workspace is a place of mutual respect — and also of considerable dark power.
Appendix B

Dress Code & Clothing Refund Procedure

+

Warlocks are expected to maintain a professional appearance in the Eldritch workplace. Guidelines:

  • Wear clean and well-maintained attire appropriate to the Eldritch workplace. Excessive revelation or offence is to be avoided.
  • Adhere to any specific dress code provided by the Patron, including required colours, symbols, or attire aligning with Eldritch powers.
  • Prior to any clothing purchases for workplace use, seek approval from the designated Eldritch representative.
  • Clothing refunds are available upon submission of a written request and receipts. The Warlock must recite the clothing oath before disbursement is authorised.

The clothing oath: "I, [Warlock's Name], solemnly swear to use the clothing purchased for the Eldritch workplace in a responsible and professional manner... So mote it be."

Appendix C

Responsible Use of Eldritch Powers

+

The Warlock is required to adhere to responsible and ethical use of their Eldritch powers. The following guidelines outline expectations:

  • Avoiding Harm: Use powers in a manner that minimises harm to others, including sentient beings, creatures, and the environment — unless the Patron instructs otherwise.
  • Respecting Consent: Obtain appropriate consent before using mind-altering spells or invocations on others.
  • Upholding Privacy: Do not use Eldritch powers to intrude upon thoughts, memories, or private information without consent.
  • Complying with Laws: Adhere to all applicable laws, including mortal, regional, and infernal regulations.
  • Using Powers for Intended Purposes: Deviation from agreed-upon uses may result in revocation of Eldritch powers.
  • Regular Review: Seek feedback from the Patron regularly. Make adjustments as required.
Appendix F

Tone Indicators in Written Communication

+

In the spirit of inclusive and respectful communication, the Warlock and Patron shall utilise tone indicators in written communication. Approved indicators include:

  • [TW] — Trigger Warning: content may be sensitive or triggering
  • [CW] — Content Warning: potentially distressing content
  • [NSFW] — Not Safe for Work: inappropriate for professional settings
  • [Humor] — Content is intended to be humorous
  • [Sarcasm] — Content is intended to be sarcastic
  • [Serious] — Content is intended to be serious or formal
  • [Half Joking] — Partially humorous with underlying seriousness
